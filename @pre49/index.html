How does the pumpless fountain work?
Elfin Fountain redefines pet fountains with the new system -NatureFlow. Every part is easy to detach and can be rinsed directly with water for effortless cleaning. And say goodbye to traditional motor pumps. Elfin Fountain employs MagDrive™ technology to dispense water, completely separating electronic components from the water source.
MagDrive™ Technology
Utilizing the MagDrive™ technology, we've meticulously positioned all electronic components outside the water tank but still inside the smart base, converting electronic power into magnetic force that drives the impeller, drawing and dispensing water.
Real water-electricity separation
By keeping all electronic parts outside the water tank, we not only extend the life of the components but also significantly increase safety when our cats are drinking water from the Elfin Fountain. Furthermore, we've streamlined the design for simplicity and ease of disassembly. Ultimately, there are only 4 components that require cleaning: the water tank, the trays, the impeller, and the water conduit
Dual water modes
Elfin Fountain features two dispensing modes: Timer Mode, dispensing water at intervals to ease your refilling concerns, and Intelligent Mode, which releases water when a pet is nearby and adjusts flow to attract them back if they've been away.
Cordless design
No need to plug in. Place your Elfin Fountain in any corner where your cat wants to drink.
Long-lasting battery
Never worry about running out of water during a power outage or when you're on a trip. All you need to do is charge it once every 30 days.
Quiet operation
With a whisper-soft sound level of just 35 dB, Elfin Fountain ensures a serene and undisturbed ambiance in your home.

FAQs
What is the 'pumpless design'?

The 'pumpless design' refers to the absence of electrical components within the water tank. Unlike other traditional pumps, the electricity from their base powers an electromagnetic actuator in those pumps, which often leads to hard-to-clean corners and challenging, costly repairs

What makes 'pumpless' design revolutionary?

Not everything that pumps water is necessarily a pump. We streamline the usually complex structures found in traditional pumps. By integrating a wireless coil into the smart base outside of the water tank, it facilitates a more thorough cleaning process, enhancing the overall maintenance experience.

What kind of pets fit in the Elfin Fountain?

The Elfin Fountain is initially and majorly designed for cats and small breeds of dogs.

How long does it take to fully charge the Elfin Fountain?

The Elfin Fountain has a battery capacity of 5000 mAh with an input of 5V⎓1A. Typically, it takes about 7 to 8 hours to fully charge.

How long can it be used once fully charged?

Once fully charged, the Elfin Fountain can last up to a month. *Usage duration might vary based on the mode and your pet's drinking frequency.
