I would give it five stars if I could adjust the Interpupillary distance (IPD).The blurry edges commented on by many users are simply due to the user's eyes being closer or further apart than the preset IPD.

In my case, my IPD is wider since if I move the glasses slightly to the left, the blurry edge will disappear on the left side, and it is the same when moving slightly to the right; the blurry edge will disappear on the right side.

It seems silly to me that there would be an issue with IPD since Rokid already accounts for an individual's personal refractive error by allowing the user to adjust the diopter per eye. Why would they account for refractive errors but not IPD in their users?

At any rate, if their next model allows the user to adjust IPD, I will pick up a pair of those. :)
