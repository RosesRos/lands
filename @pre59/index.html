FAQs
HOW LONG DO THEY LAST?

Each person walks, weighs, and uses their sneakers differently, so varies, but they last for more than 30 days of continuous use.

WILL I SLIP?

No, you will not slip. In fact, the PEVA technology adds an element of extra grip. Yes, even when it's raining, but even regular rubber shoes still slip in the rain, so please keep that in mind.

WILL IT FIT MY SNEAKER?

Yes, it fits on any rubber/mesh you can think of, up to a men's size 14 US. Yeezy's, Jordans, Air Force 1's, you name it. If you have doubts about the sole protector sticking to your shoe, heat it up for a tad longer than normal, that way, it sticks to the crevices better. If you still have doubts about having a larger shoe, the sheets are 15 inches long and 6 inches wide, so you can measure your shoe to make sure it will fit. Feel free to reach out with any concerns so we can help you out.

HOW DO I APPLY IT?

Trace, cut, clean, peel, apply, heat, and press. It‚Äôs that simple! The application process takes about five minutes per pair.

  1. Trace with a pen or pencil around your shoe while it‚Äôs placed in the middle of the sheet, with the white paper facing upwards. Leave some extra room if you want the protector to go around the midsole.
  2. Cut along the line while keeping the edges smooth.
  3. Clean the soles of your sneakers with the included alcohol wipes, even if your sneakers are brand new.
  4. Peel the paper off and carefully place it onto the sneaker. Try to minimize touching the adhesive. The fewer touches, the better it will stick.
  5. Apply lots of pressure.
  6. Apply heat with a heat gun or hair dryer until the sole protector is engraved into the crevasses of the sole and the plastic is slightly warped.
  7. Trim any unwanted excess plastic sticking out from the sides of the sneaker.
  8. Repeat with other sneaker(s), and enjoy wearing your sneakers without worrying.
WILL IT RUIN MY SNEAKERS?

No, the adhesive is residue-free when you peel it off after it starts to wear through.
